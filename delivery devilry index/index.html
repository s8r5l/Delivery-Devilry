Delivery Devilry

Magical Package Carriers

Inspired by Kiki’s Delivery Service, Delivery Devilry is a board game where you and your friends play as magical couriers employed by competing delivery services. Make deliveries, build your service’s reputation, and disrupt the other players to earn the coveted Golden Parcel.

Chose between a Cultist, Shaman, Witch, or Wizard and use a combination of class skills, magical powers, and luck to become the best parcel service in the city.
